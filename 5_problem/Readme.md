# 📊 Data Science Challenge — Customer Behaviour Analysis

## 🎯 Objective

You are hired as a **Junior Data Scientist** in a retail analytics company.

The company wants to understand customer behaviour and spending patterns using the provided dataset.

You are required to perform:

* Data Cleaning
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Outlier Detection
* Hypothesis Testing

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

* Total Records: **10,000**
* Dataset Type: **Mixed (Numerical + Categorical + Discrete)**
* Dataset contains:

  * Missing Values
  * Outliers
  * Multiple customer behaviour features

---

## 🧠 Business Context

The company wants answers to questions like:

* Do higher income customers spend more?
* Does online purchasing behaviour impact spending score?
* Does customer location tier influence income or spending?

Your analysis will help the business make marketing and product strategy decisions.

---

## ✅ Tasks

### 🔹 Task 1 — Data Cleaning

* Identify missing values in dataset
* Apply suitable imputation strategy
* Detect and handle inappropriate or inconsistent values
* Encode categorical features if required

---

### 🔹 Task 2 — Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Study distribution of numerical variables
* Identify skewness and spread
* Perform both:

  * Visual EDA
  * Numerical EDA
* Discover patterns, relationships, and associations between variables

---

### 🔹 Task 3 — Outlier Detection

* Detect outliers using suitable statistical methods
* Decide appropriate treatment:

  * Removal
  * Capping
  * Retention

Provide reasoning for chosen strategy.

---

### 🔹 Task 4 — Hypothesis Testing

Frame and test **at least TWO statistical hypotheses** based on business questions.

For each hypothesis:

* Define Null Hypothesis (H₀)
* Define Alternate Hypothesis (H₁)
* Choose Significance Level (SL)
* Select appropriate statistical test
* Compute p-value
* Make decision and conclude

---

## 📌 Final Deliverables

* Cleaned dataset insights
* Key EDA observations
* Outlier handling explanation
* Hypothesis testing results
* Business interpretation of findings

---

## ⚠️ Rules

* Do not assume results without statistical validation
* Provide logical reasoning for each step
* Focus on real business understanding
* Maintain reproducible analysis workflow

---

## 🚀 Goal

Develop practical thinking in:

* Data Cleaning
* Statistical EDA
* Outlier reasoning
* Hypothesis driven decision making

This challenge simulates a **real entry-level Data Scientist assignment**.
